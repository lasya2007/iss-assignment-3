My Hometown

I am from Rajamahendravaram, a picturesque city on the banks of the river Godavari in Andhra Pradesh, often referred to as the cultural capital of the state. It is celebrated for its rich heritage and is believed to be the birthplace of the Telugu language, with the esteemed poet Nannayya hailing from this region. The city is also home to the iconic Godavari Bridge—an engineering marvel that gracefully spans the river, symbolizing Rajahmundry's blend of history and modern progress.
